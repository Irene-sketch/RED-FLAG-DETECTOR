Red Flags vs. Green Flags

Identify behaviors and signs that indicate healthy (green flags) or unhealthy (red flags) relationships.

Green Flag Behavior Red Flag Behavior
Open and honest communication Frequent lying or withholding information
Respect for boundaries Pressuring or ignoring personal boundaries
Supportive and encouraging Criticizing or putting you down often
Taking responsibility for actions Blaming others for personal mistakes
Equal give and take in relationships Controlling or dominating behavior
